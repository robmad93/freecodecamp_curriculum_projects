Glory for Clare: Champions of Ireland

"Glory for Clare: Champions of Ireland" recounts Clare's epic journey to win the All-Ireland hurling final in 2024. The movie culminates in the heart-racing clash between Cork and Clare, with Clare emerging as victors to claim the Liam McCarthy Cup.
