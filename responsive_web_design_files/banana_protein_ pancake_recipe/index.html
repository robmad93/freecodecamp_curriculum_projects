Banana protein pancake recipe

Everybody goes bananas for pancakes! So why not combine bananas with pancakes? This recipe will win the hearts of all gym gorillas in your household!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 pint milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 banana
  • A dash of vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 scoop banana whey protein
  • 2 tbsp stevia (optional)

Instructions

  1. Mash the banana in a mixing bowl.
  2. Put the flour, whey protein, and baking soda into the bowl.
  3. Break the eggs into the bowl.
  4. Slowly add the milk to the bowl, ensuring to whisk the mixture as you pour.
  5. Add a dash of vanilla extract.
  6. Add 2 or more tbsp of stevia to sweeten further (optional).
  7. Once the mixture is smooth, leave it to settle in a fridge for at least 30 minutes.
  8. Heat some olive oil in a pancake pan, ensuring the surface is coated with oil. Remove excess oil from the pan once it is heated.
  9. Gently pour the pancake mixture into the pan until 3/4 of the base surface is covered.
  10. Cook the pancake until the mixture begins to solidify.
  11. Once the underside is cooked, flip the pankcake to cook the top side.
  12. Serve the pancake with some banana slices and any toppings of your choice. Bon appetit!
