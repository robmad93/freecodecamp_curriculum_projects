The Clare Courier

Cliffs of Moher Remains a Beloved Tourist Site for Domestic Tourists

Tourists and locals alike have once again confirmed what everyone in Co. Clare already knew: the Cliffs of Moher remain “fair impressive altogether.” Despite strong winds, sideways rain, and one lost woolly hat, visitors described the experience as “worth it, to be fair.”

Ennis Traffic Jam Caused by Ducks Crossing Road

Morning commuters were delayed for nearly four minutes yesterday when a family of ducks decided to take their time crossing the Mill Road. This same family of ducks delayed commuters for five minutes one month prior. According to one local resident: "They've managed to shave one minute off their time, fair play to them."

Local Pub Introduces “One More Won't Kill You” Policy

A well-known Clare pub has officially extended closing time by popular demand. Management confirmed the decision was made after hearing the phrase “Ah go on” over 300 times in one evening.

Weather Forecast: Rain, Followed by More Rain

Met Éireann has boldly predicted rain across the county, with occasional dry spells designed to trick people into leaving the house without a coat. Umbrellas remain “no use whatsoever.”

County Clare Man Fixes Everything with Duct Tape

A local hero has reportedly repaired a gate, a chair, and a satellite dish using only duct tape. Experts believe it is "only a matter of time" before everything falls apart.
