Local Man Declares “Grand Stretch” After Five Minutes of Sunshine
County Clare residents cautiously optimistic as jackets briefly removed before being put straight back on
By Robert Madigan

Scenes of wild celebration erupted across County Clare yesterday when the sun appeared for a full five minutes shortly after noon. Eyewitnesses report at least three people saying “it’s fierce mild altogether” while one daring individual in Ennis was seen leaving the house without a coat, only to regret it deeply moments later.

Local experts have confirmed that the phenomenon, known scientifically as “winter fatigue,” represents a coping strategy adopted by individuals to deal with the uneventful and often bleak period that follows the Christmas holidays. Meanwhile, shops reported a spike in the sale of sunglasses, followed immediately by refunds, as heavy clouds returned and normal service resumed. At press time, residents had agreed it was “still bright enough out” despite standing in near darkness.
